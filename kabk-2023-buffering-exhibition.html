Cosmic Reverie

Year: 2023
BUFFERING, LOADING, PENDING, BECOMING, GENERATING…
Group Exhibition
The Royal Academy of Art, The Hague
Technique & Material: Moving image, 3D animation, 70” monitor
Guided by Anna Hoetjes

In this poetic exploration of a mysterious and awe-inspiring planet beyond our reach, we delve into the essence of consciousness and the intricacies of the universe. Forces surge and consciousness flows, creating a puzzle that eludes our understanding. As we journey through this cosmic creation, the planet’s consciousness is a reminder that life is fleeting and a chance to grow, learn, and be in harmony with the immensity of the universe. But as we contemplate the wonders of this planet, we must also consider its future. How will our actions impact Its fate?
